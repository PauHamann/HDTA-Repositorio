JOHN DEFILIPPO Top Junior Development Coach

ABOUT

John DeFilippo is the tennis director at Solaris Racquet Club and leads the Hirscht DeFilippo Tennis Academy, which is dedicated to providing a training ground for players to reach their highest potential. For the past 30 years, DeFilippo has been managing the academy in the area. According to DeFilippo, the academy is recognized by colleges for producing top-level players who go on to compete at higher levels.

”Making a difference in a kid's life is more important than material or financial reward”

He has been one of the top junior development coaches in the Northeast for over 30 years and has placed hundreds of junior players in the best colleges in the country.

"I feel if a kid trains right and they are on the court 10-12 hours a week, they can live in their own home, sleep in their own bed, get a good education that the northeast provides and they can accomplish as much."
